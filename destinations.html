Maldives Luxury Resort Overview?
The Maldives is home to a multitude of luxury resorts and coastal hotels and is world famous for emphasising the one island, one resort concept. This concept also extend to each island’s house reef, not every island has one where you can snorkel / dive. This list of resorts is each surrounded by magnificent house reef. Ideally packed with tropical corals and a variety of aquatic life.
How are the lagoon resorts furnished?
Lagoon Suite Pool, Cocoon Maldives In this one of the most exclusive resort in the Maldives, the lagoon suite with pool offers unique accommodation settings with a private pool and stylish Italian design.
Do you have beach bungalow's?
Hidden in the native gardens of Dhigali, a perfect getaway for a romantic holiday. A wide covered veranda leading to porcelain sands with isolated sun loungers to relax. The beach bungalow is steps away from the shore and overlooks the crystal turquiose waters of Dhigali’s lagoon.
